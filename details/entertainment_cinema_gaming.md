# Entertainment, Cinema, & Gaming Pre-Visualization Production

Allows film and game studios to apply custom hand-drawn or stylized aesthetics to 3D and video renders.

## Core Concept
- Eliminates manual rotoscaping loops.
- Automatically renders scenes matching specific artistic profiles.
